Old Trafford is a football stadium in Old Trafford, Trafford, Greater Manchester, United Kingdom. This is the home ground of Manchester United football club. With a capacity of 74,140 people,[1] it is the largest club-owned football stadium (and second largest football stadium after Wembley Stadium) in the United Kingdom, and the second largest. 11 in Europe.[2] The ground is located approximately 800 meters from Old Trafford Cricket Ground and a nearby tram stop.

  • The highest number of spectators at Old Trafford was about 76,098 spectators, at a match between Manchester United and Blackburn Rovers in the Premier League on 31 March 2007. This is also a Premier League record attendance. League.
  • The highest average attendance at Old Trafford in a season was 75,826 spectators in the 2006–07 season.
  • The lowest average attendance at Old Trafford in a season was 11,685 spectators in the 1930-1931 season.
